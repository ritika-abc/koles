About Us

Bardhaman Agro Products (I) Pvt. Ltd.

Bardhaman Agro Products (I) Pvt. Ltd. (BAPPL) is a leading name in the rice manufacturing industry, dedicated to delivering premium quality rice products to our valued customers. BAPPL was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal- to bring the best rice to every home. With a rich heritage and a commitment to excellence, we take pride in being a trusted provider of superior rice grains. The company was established as a partnership firm in 2001. Later, in 2009, BAPPL was incorporated as a private limited company. Since 2001, BAPPL has been at the forefront of rice manufacturing. Our journey began with a small mill nested in the heart of fertile farmland, where we embraced the art of traditional rice milling techniques. BAPPL has grown into a renowned brand due to our uncompromising quality and customer satisfaction. We serve our products across India and in some foreign countries like UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, & Qatar.

At BAPPL, we understand that rice is more than just a food staple; it's a reflection of culture, heritage, and taste. That is why, the finest grains meticulously from selected farms that meet our rigorous standards of quality and sustainability. Our state-of-the-art processing facilities employ advanced technologies to ensure that each grain of rice is cleaned, milled, and packaged with utmost precision, preserving its natural flavour, aroma, and nutritional value. Our brand portfolios, those are available across India include Rose Brand, Double Rose, Three Rose, Four Rose, Seven Rose, Three Maan Brand, and Basumoti Rice. On the other hand, Bardhaman Rose is one of the brands of BAPPL which is available across the international markets. We believe in responsible manufacturing practices that not only benefit our customers but also contribute to the well-being of the environment and the community we operate in. Further, through sustainable agricultural methods, water conservation initiatives, and waste reduction strategies, we strive to minimize our ecological footprint. In addition to this, your health and safety are of paramount importance to us. At BAPPL, we adhere to strict food safety regulations and maintain rigorous quality control measures throughout our manufacturing process. Therefore, we sell only ISO 9001-2015, ISO 22000-2018, Halal, HACCP, FSSAI, SFDA and APEDA-certified products to ensure the maintaining quality standards. Also, with BAPPL, by your side, you can be assured of the finest rice grain that elevates your dining experience to new heights.

 

"To be the leading provider of high-quality rice products offering a diverse range of grains that meet the evolving needs and preferences of our customers. We strive to uphold the values of integrity, sustainability, and customer satisfaction while promoting the rich cultural heritage and nutritional benefits of rice across various countries."
